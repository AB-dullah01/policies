Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2025

If you connected your Facebook account to Marketing Minds and wish to delete your data, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "Marketing Minds".
  4. Click "Remove".
  5. Your data will be deleted from our servers within 24 hours.

For manual deletion requests, email: opslybusiness@gmail.com
